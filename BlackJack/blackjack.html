BlackJack Rules

  • Goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over (AKA busting).
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) and 10 are worth 10 points each.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11 points (whichever is better for the hand).
  • Dealer hits until 17 or higher.
  • A blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2.
